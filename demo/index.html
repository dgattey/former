Former alpha

A project by Dylan Gattey available on Github

Sick of losing everything in a form because you accidentally navigated away? Wish you could go back to a previous version of an input or text field? Former is here to rescue you with automatic, timeline-based backups, saved right to your browser.

Demo

Former is supported on both the textareas and the input fields of type text of any form. Try typing into either one of those and restoring from an older edit!
